Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my tasks across devices?
Sign in with your account on all devices. Your tasks will automatically sync in real-time when you're connected to the internet.
How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select Chronos
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
You'll continue to have premium access until the end of your billing period.

Can I use Chronos offline?
Yes! All your tasks are cached locally, so you can view and edit them offline. Changes will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.
How do I delete my account?

  • Advanced analytics and insights
  • Custom task categories
  • Data export functionality
  • Priority customer support
